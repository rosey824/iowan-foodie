Iowa's First Food Truck Park

April 7th, 2022

The Parc at Community Choice

Ames, IA - Iowa is set to welcome its first dedicated food truck park with the grand opening of The Parc in Ames this weekend. Located in the heart of downtown Ames, The Parc aims to offer a unique dining experience, featuring a variety of high-quality food trucks from the region.

The Parc will showcase a diverse selection of food trucks, serving everything from gourmet burgers to vegan options. This new venue is designed to provide a vibrant community space where visitors can enjoy different cuisines in a relaxed and social atmosphere.

Zaid Gustafson, an Iowa State University alum and park manager with Community Choice Credit Union, emphasized the significance of the new park. “Being the first food truck park in Iowa was kind of a big deal for us and hopefully a big deal for Iowa and Ames,” Gustafson said. “This was for the Ames community.”

Located conveniently in downtown Ames, The Parc features ample seating and shaded areas, making it a perfect spot for casual dining and community gatherings. The grand opening will include live music and special promotions, with the park open to the public at no cost.
