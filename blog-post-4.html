Swed & Co Coffee

May 24th, 2023

Fort Madison, IA - Swed and Co. quickly became a beloved staple in Fort Madison, Iowa, offering a cozy retreat just steps away from the Mississippi River. This café is more than just a place to grab a coffee; it’s a lively community hub where delicious food, friendly faces, and scenic views all come together. (Yeah, that includes the beautiful view of latte art on the cup of coffee sitting in front of me.)

From the moment you step into Swed and Co., you’re embraced by a warm and inviting atmosphere. The interior features a mix of rustic and modern decor, with wooden tables, comfortable seating, and large windows that fill the space with natural light. The decor reflects the town’s feel, creating a cozy ambiance perfect for a relaxing cup of coffee or catching up with friends.

Behind the counter at Swed and Co., you'll find a glimpse into the cafe’s charm: coffee menu wall display, a record player playing vinyl, showcasing their diverse range of brews, alongside a tempting array of freshly baked pastries served with a warm smile. The blend of vibrant colors and presentation makes it clear that every detail at Swed and Co. is crafted with care and passion.

But it’s not just about the aesthetics. Swed and Co. prides itself on its commitment to quality and community. The staff is known for their warm hospitality, always ready with a smile and a recommendation. Whether you're a local regular or a first-time visitor, the welcoming atmosphere invites you to linger and enjoy.

Experience Swed & Co.

Whether you’re looking for a cozy spot to enjoy your morning coffee, a delicious lunch with friends, or a peaceful place to unwind, Swed and Co. truly has something special to offer.
