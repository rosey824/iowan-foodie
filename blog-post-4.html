Swed & Co Coffee

May 24th, 2023

Fort Madison, IA -Swed and Co. quickly became a beloved staple in Fort Madison, Iowa, offering a cozy retreat just steps away from the Mississippi River. This café is more than just a place to grab a coffee; it’s a lively community hub where delicious food, friendly faces, and scenic views all come together. (Yeah, that includes the beautiful view of latte art on the cup of coffee sitting in front of me.)

From the moment you step into Swed and Co., you’re embraced by a warm and inviting atmosphere. The interior features mix of rustic and modern decor, with wooden tables, comfortable seating, and large windows that fill the space with natural light. The decor reflects the town’s feel, creating a cozy ambiance perfect for a relaxing cup of coffee or catch-up with friends.

Experience Swed & Co.

Whether you’re looking for a cozy spot to enjoy your morning coffee, a delicious lunch with friends, or a peaceful place to unwind, Swed and Co. truly has something special to offer.

Want to learn more about Swed and Co. or share your experience? Visit their official website for more information and updates.
