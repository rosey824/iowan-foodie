Cinnaholic Des Moines Grand Opening

April 7th, 2022

Des Moines, IA - Cinnaholic Des Moines is set to open its doors to the public tomorrow, April 8th, at 10 AM. Located in the East Village of Des Moines, this new addition offers a unique twist on a beloved classic: cinnamon rolls.

Cinnaholic is renowned for its commitment to serving 100% vegan treats. All of their offerings are dairy-free, lactose-free, and egg-free, making them accessible to a wide range of customers, including those with dietary restrictions. The brand gained national recognition after being featured on the hit show Shark Tank, where it impressed the judges with its delicious and innovative concept.

During their private soft opening event held last night, attendees were treated to a preview of the bakery’s delectable cinnamon rolls. The consensus? These rolls are nothing short of spectacular.

“WOW, these cinnamon rolls are AMAZING — a dream come true for all the sweets lovers out there,” one guest exclaimed.

To celebrate the grand opening, Cinnaholic Des Moines will offer a special promotion: from 10 AM to 2 PM on Friday, April 8th, all cinnamon rolls will be available for just $1, with a limit of one roll per person. This limited-time offer is expected to draw a large crowd, eager to taste these vegan delights.

Cinnaholic Des Moines is sure to become a popular destination for both locals and visitors, offering indulgent and flavorful cinnamon rolls. You have to stop by and experience these incredible treats for yourself.

Grand Opening Event Details

Friday, April 8th, 10 am - 2 pm

505 E Grand Ave Suite 101, Des Moines, IA 50309

Special Offer:

$1 cinnamon rolls (limit one per customer)
