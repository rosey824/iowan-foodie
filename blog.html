A Sweet Experience at Cinnaholic Des Moines

Posted on April 7th, 2022 by Emma Rose

Hey, fellow foodies! I had the opportunity to attend the soft opening of Cinnaholic Des Moines and I am beyond excited to share my experience with you all. If you love cinnamon rolls as much as I do, you'll want to mark your calendars for their grand opening tomorrow, April 8th, at 10 AM. Located in the vibrant East Village of Des Moines, Cinnaholic offers a unique and mouth-watering selection of cinnamon rolls that are 100% vegan. Yep, you read that right. Everything they serve is dairy-free, lactose-free, and egg-free, making it a fantastic treat for everyone. During the private event, I got to taste these heavenly rolls, and all I can say is... WOW! The flavors are incredible, and each bite is a dream come true for any sweets lover. The rolls are perfectly soft, gooey, and packed with flavor. To make the grand opening even sweeter, there's a special deal from 10 AM to 2 PM on April 8th: all cinnamon rolls will be $1, with a limit of one roll per person. Trust me, you do not want to miss out on this delicious deal! So, head over to Cinnaholic Des Moines tomorrow and treat yourself to the best cinnamon rolls in the East Village. You won't regret it!.
